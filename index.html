Frequently asked questions:

How does car shipping work?

Car shipping involves hiring a reputable carrier to transport your vehicle from one location to another. As a car shipping brokerage company, we connect you with reliable carriers who specialize in vehicle transportation. They handle the logistics, including pickup, transportation, and delivery, ensuring your vehicle reaches its destination safely.

How long does car shipping take?

The transit time for car shipping can vary based on several factors, including the distance, route, weather conditions, and carrier availability. Typically, car shipping within the contiguous United States takes 5-10 days. However, these estimates are subject to change, and we recommend discussing specific timelines with the carrier handling your shipment.

Is my vehicle insured during transportation?

Yes, carriers are required to carry insurance coverage for the vehicles they transport. However, the extent of coverage may vary but we always review the carrier's insurance policy to make sure your vehicle is delivered safe and sound. In case of any damages during transportation, it is important to report them immediately to the carrier and our customer service team.

How do I prepare my vehicle for shipping?

To prepare your vehicle for shipping, ensure it is clean, remove personal belongings, and secure any loose items. Document the existing condition of your vehicle by taking photographs and making a note of any pre-existing damages. Additionally, make sure the fuel tank is around one-quarter full, and provide a set of keys to the carrier.

Can I ship personal items inside my vehicle?

It is generally not recommended to ship personal items inside your vehicle during car transportation. Carriers are primarily licensed to transport vehicles, not personal belongings. However, some carriers may allow a small number of lightweight and securely packed items inside the vehicle. It's best to discuss this with the carrier beforehand.

How do I track the progress of my shipment?

We provide tracking updates throughout the transportation process. Once your vehicle is assigned to a carrier, you will receive their contact information. You can directly communicate with the carrier to obtain updates on the status and location of your shipment. If you have any concerns or require additional assistance, our customer service team is here to help.

Can I ship my vehicle if it is not running?

Yes, we can assist with shipping non-running vehicles. However, please notify us in advance so that we can find a carrier equipped to handle such transportation. Nonrunning vehicles may require additional equipment or arrangements, which may impact the pricing and logistics of the shipment.

How do I cancel or change my car shipping order?

To cancel or change your car shipping order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. We will assist you in making the necessary adjustments. However, please note that cancellation or modification fees may apply depending on the timing and circumstances of the request.

How can I pay for car shipping services?

Payment terms and options may vary depending on the carrier and the specifics of your shipment. Typically, carriers accept payment via credit/debit cards, bank transfers, or cash on delivery (COD). The payment details and arrangements will be discussed and agreed upon between you and the carrier prior to finalizing the shipment.

What if my vehicle gets damaged during transportation?

In the unlikely event of any damages during transportation, it is crucial to report them immediately to the carrier and our customer service team. Take photographs and document the damages. The carrier's insurance should cover eligible claims. We will assist you in communicating with the carrier and resolving any issues that may arise.

If you have any further questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our customer service team, and we will be glad to assist you!
